My Love for Flowers & Preserving Them

I have always loved flowers. I love their beauty, their symbolism, and the memories they hold.

Preserving flowers lets me keep those memories forever.

How I Preserve Flowers

  1. Choose the flowers
  2. Prepare them for pressing
    • Remove moisture
    • Flatten petals
    • Arrange carefully
  3. Press and preserve
    • Place flowers in between pages of a book
    • Place book under something heavy
    • Leave for 2-3 weeks or until fully dried
